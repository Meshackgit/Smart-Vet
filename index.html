ABOUT US

Smart Vet is a full animal clinic located along Ngong road in Adams, Nairobi county.
We deal with vaccinations, check-ups, nutrition and grooming of animals.

The mission of Smart Vet is to provide the highest standard of preventive and medical care to your animals companions.

At Smart Vet, we treat your pets like the valued family members they
are- professionally and respectfully.

Our Services

Smart-vet is the leader of the pack when it comes to the absolute
best care for your animals.

Vaccination

Vaccination has long been an effective way to reduce disease burden in pets and farm animals, and is a key tool in maintaining animal health and welfare.

Check-ups

Our check-up's are meant to allow the owner of the animals track the animal's health and know where he or she is supposed to work on regarding their animal's current health status.

Relocation

Are you moving cross country and don't know how you are going to fit your pet in the car along with the rest of the family?

Grooming

Our grooming package includes spa bath, nail clipping, coat treaming, sanitary trim, brush out and more others.

Day care

Our Dog Daycare is tailor-made to give your dog the best experience together with some socialization training. Every session is supervised by our trained Pets Paradise care givers and every dog is taken care of accordingly.

Pet boarding

We are committed to ensuring that every pet feels relaxed and secure while in our care. Because no dog is quite like yours, we offer a la carte services to add to our standard boarding package for a fully customized stay.

Nutrition

From their days as puppies and kittens to their years as senior dogs and cats, our biology-based nutrition stays a step ahead for differences you can see, feel and trust.

Diagnostics

Our highly trained experts perform many different laboratory tests that
help treat animals and provide safe food for animals for good health.
